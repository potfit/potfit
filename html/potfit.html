Authors

potfit was originally developed by Peter Brommer during his diploma thesis work.

The development of potfit is continued by Peter Brommer as part of his PhD thesis work. This work is funded through the DFG, Sonderforschungsbereich 382 "Simulation physikalischer Prozesse auf Hochleistungsrechnern", Teilprojekt C14 "Molekulardynamik von Quasikristallen"

You are free to use potfit within the limits set by the licence, but I request you acknowledge its use by citing the relevant publications on this program.
